About Me

I graduated from highschool in 2023 and was immediately interested in Computer Sciences. I enrolled into Keyin College's Software Development program in January, 2024. Software Development is a skill that i am grateful for and that I utilize every day. It is rewarding, exciting, and certainly frusterating at times but there is no feeling comparable to overcoming errors and obstacles and perfecting a project.

I aspire to be employed in the front end development field so that I can unleash my creativity and add to my portfolio each day.

I grew up in a rural town in the small province of Newfoundland, and I hope one day to be an inspiration to other girls and women in STEM.
